**Answer1**- Sexual harassment isnt just about big things; its any unwelcome behavior—like uncomfortable touching, suggestive joke, or prying into someone private life that make a colleague feel unsafe or 
belittled. Whether its a inappropriate comment about how someone look or a "favor" asked in exchange for a promotion, if the behavior is uninvited and create a heavy, hostile atmosphere, its harassment. At 
its core, its about a lack of respect for person and professional boundaries.

**Answer2**- If you ever find yourself in this situation or see it happen to a teammate, try to speak up and tell the person to stop if you feel safe doing so. Its important to keep a private log of what 
happened the "who, what, and when" and then reach out to the Internal Complaints Committee or HR. Reporting isnt just about follow rules, its about looking out for yourself and your colleague to ensure the 
office remain a space where everyone actually feel comfortable showing up to work.
